Privacy Policy & OpSec

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Our Commitment to Anonymity

We operate on a strict Need-To-Know basis. The system does not request, enforce, or store legal real names. All data is linked to an encrypted internal System ID.

We believe privacy is a fundamental human right. AceGirlsKE is built on a "Zero-Knowledge" storage principle. We only collect the absolute minimum data required to facilitate the technological operation of the platform and to ensure basic community safety.

2. Ephemeral Data & Verification

To maintain a high-trust environment, we mandate identity matching verification for advertisers. However, we do not archive these materials.

  • Verification photos and videos are strictly used for initial identity matching to prevent catfishing and impersonation.
  • Once an account is approved, the verification media is treated as Ephemeral Data and is permanently purged from our servers within 24 hours. We do not keep historical records of verification materials.

3. No Tracking, No Selling

We are not a data broker. We do not use intrusive tracking cookies, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting logic to follow your activity across the internet.

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data, phone numbers, or analytics to third parties.
  • Any analytics collected are exclusively for internal performance optimization and combating spam or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks.
  • We use strictly functional cookies required to maintain login sessions and security states.

4. The Right to Erasure (Data Deletion)

You have absolute control over your footprint on this platform. In compliance with modern Data Protection Acts, including the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019):

Any independent contractor listed on the platform can exercise their Right to Erasure at any moment. By using the /delete_account command in our Telegram bot or clicking "Delete Account" via the portal interface, your profile, photos, and associated identifier records will be instantly and irreversibly wiped from the active database.

5. Data Jurisdiction

To ensure maximum operational security and resistance to unwarranted censorship or localized data breaches, our primary servers and databases are hosted in privacy-respecting offshore jurisdictions. We utilize end-to-end encryption for sensitive intra-server communications.
